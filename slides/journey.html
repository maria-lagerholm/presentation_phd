The PhD Journey

The road from start to defence was anything but a straight line

Skills ↑

analytical skills · writing · research · grasping new concepts · critical thinking · discipline · focus

Certainty ↓

how much I am sure I understand how the world works

Looking back, the path from the first day of my PhD to this defence was not a straight line at all. It looped, climbed, dropped, and doubled back many times, but it still arrived at the final point.

Along the way, two opposite trends emerged. My analytical skills, writing, research ability, capacity to grasp new concepts, critical thinking, discipline, and focus all went steadily up.

At the same time, my certainty about how much I truly understand the world went steadily down. The more I learned, the more I realised how much remains unknown.
